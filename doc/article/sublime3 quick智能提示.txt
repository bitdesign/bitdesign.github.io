1. 安装PackegeControl
下载 https://sublime.wbond.net/Package%20Control.sublime-package
将Package Control.sublime-package粘贴到Sublime3安装目录的Packages文件夹，例如：C:\Program Files (x86)\Sublime Text 3\Packages
重启Sublime3

2.安装QuickXDev
Preferences--Packege Control然后选择Packege Control：Install Packege
输入QuickXDev
等待完成安装即可
